POEM-Tastic! brings a collection of poems written throughout the previous months, that makes you remember of everyday things at home, at the office, or even at school! Hoping that these poems will leave a POEM-Tastic! experience to remember.


3 Steps To Enjoy the Poems:

  1. Unmute the music on the top of the webpage, to set the mood.
  2. Scroll down. If you want to go back to the top, just click on the website banner on the top.
  3. Read the poems. Don't forget to enjoy the pictures that come with it.

Menu

At School
See poems about things as small as a pencil, or as big as a classroom. Remember school with poems about school.
At Home
See poems about things as small as a pair of slippers, or as big as a door. Remember your home with poems about home.
At the City
See poems about things from the pedestrian lane you cross to the city you live in. Remember your city with poems about the city.
At the Outdoors
See poems about things as near as the rain, or as far as the stars. Remember nature with poems about the outdoors.
