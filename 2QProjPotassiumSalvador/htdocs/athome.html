POEM-Tastic! brings a collection of poems written throughout the months, that makes you remember of everyday things at home, at the office, or even at school! Hoping that these poems will leave a POEM-Tastic! experience to remember.


Below here are 10 poems about things at home. Together are some pictures to help you reminisce more about times spent at home. Enjoy!

Monthly Calendar

There are 12 in a year
One for each month
Use it, or not
It may be good for scheduling.

Green Plants

It may be everywhere
You may see it right at your door
They bring more than what meets the eye!
They support our entire world!

Rubber Slippers

To make your feet warm
From the different variations of land
To serve as protection
For you to continue walking on...

Porcelain Plates

One of the things used to eat
Maybe fragile; it breaks easily
But it contains the food
That will be eaten soon.

Sturdy Chair

Whether it be wood or plastic
It gives relief to a tired one
It may break sometimes
But everyone uses it.

Light Bulb

Powered by electricity
It lights up the rooms
It lights up the streets
Especially when the moon is up.

Flashing Television

Once black and white
And now filled with colors
Entertainment is always there
Ready to give smiles and laughter.

Solid Door

Whether it be tall or short
Narrow or wide
It can be seen everywhere
It helps in entering and exiting a room.

White Toilet

Always in the restroom
Always ready to take poop and pee
And with just one flush
It gulps all of them in!

Soft Pillow

Whatever shape it is
It is soft and made of cotton
You hug it, your rest on it
Bading another day goodbye.
