POEM-Tastic! brings a collection of poems written throughout the months, that makes you remember of everyday things at home, at the office, or even at school! Hoping that these poems will leave a POEM-Tastic! experience to remember.


Below here are 10 poems about things at the outdoors. Together are some pictures to help you reminisce more about times spent outdoors. Enjoy!

Cold Precipitation

Whether it be rain or snow
Hail or sleet
They all fall from the sky
Always ushering the cold times.

Yellow Sun

It lights up our days
And holds up the planets together
A star it is called
And will remain for a long time so.

White Moon

It lights up our nights
When we are going to fall asleep
But did you ever know?
Itâ€™s light came from the Sun.

Distant Stars

One in the day
But many in the night
They are all far away
But are always bright in the sky.

Black Night

Starts when the Sun sets
Starts when the Moon is high in the sky.
Stars shining their light
Such a sight to behold!

Tall Mountains

They are tall, and sometimes with snow
They're in many countries in the world
They provide good sights at the top
Together with a good hiking experience.

Flowing River

Long, winding roads of water
That started early civilizations
A source of life
It keeps trade going on.

Wide Plains

Where the horizon is seen
Such a vast piece of land
Many plants are planted there
From vegetables to fruits.

Guiding Map

Sometimes, going out needs a map
Where it gives the way
Either in paper or phone
They are quite reliable.

Dangerous Volcano

Sometimes active, sometimes dormant
But both are very dangerous
At any time, with little warning
They will erupt, and spew out hot lava.
