POEM-Tastic! brings a collection of poems written throughout the months, that makes you remember of everyday things at home, at the office, or even at school! Hoping that these poems will leave a POEM-Tastic! experience to remember.


Below here are 10 poems about things at school. Together are some pictures to help you reminisce more about times spent in school. Enjoy!

Lost Objects

Whether an eraser that dropped out to nowhere
Or a ballpen that slid underneath
A loss incurred, in such a fine day
Will it be found? I hope it does.

School Notebook

A couple of pages
Bound into one piece
Very well for writing
In school, and even in work.

Old Book

Written by someone in the past
With information yet to behold
“Cough, cough!” I say
As I dwell into its pages.

New Pencil

Just bought from a bookstore
Is it ready for it’s noble job?
I hope so
It will write so much stuff; so many things!

Faithful Eraser

An object so faithful
It sacrifices itself
Its form, its beauty
Just to correct someone’s mistakes.

Graphing Paper

With lines that criss-cross each other
Forming grids across
It is fun to graph in it
Either in Math or in Science.

Number Line

An infinite line
With any kind of number
Except for the complex ones, though
They are for another story.

School Day

It starts and ends with a bell
Where children and teachers meet
A day full of activities
A day full of learning.

One Hundred

Ten times ten
Results a number quite big
A new place value it introduces
A new set of numbers it gives.

Class Room

With seats everywhere
That is modified to have desks
Many students enter and exit
With knowledge they always bring.
