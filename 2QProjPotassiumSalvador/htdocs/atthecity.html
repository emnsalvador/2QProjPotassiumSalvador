POEM-Tastic! brings a collection of poems written throughout the months, that makes you remember of everyday things at home, at the office, or even at school! Hoping that these poems will leave a POEM-Tastic! experience to remember.


Below here are 10 poems about things at the city. Together are some pictures to help you reminisce more about times spent in the city. Enjoy!

Big Trucks

Huge, with lots of loads
Everyone sits aside when it passes over
It can break roads
But keeps the economy alive.

Movie House

With lots of seats
Lights off, just screen and sound
With popcorn and some drinks, maybe
It is prepared to show a movie.

Winding Roads

No matter how many lanes
Or how many cars pass through
They connect cities, towns, and areas
And makes travel easier.

Sturdy Bridge

It connects separated lands
And lets everything cross on it
A very strong structure
That can maybe, last for years.

Busy City

An area quite crowded
With buildings and houses in every corner
Such a sight to behold
With all the traffic, all the sounds!

Efficient Bus

A vehicle quite huge
With many seats inside
Such an efficient vehicle
It can reduce traffic in the streets.

Pedestrian Lane

Marked by bars across
Where people cross along
And when itâ€™s time though
It will be passed by vehicles.

Stop Light

With colors of red, yellow, and green
It controls traffic in the roads
In time, people will cross
In time, vehicles will cross.

Historical Museum

A building, sometimes small or huge
Featuring many artifacts and arts
Such history, from very different times
Housed in just one building.

Green Park

A good space in the city
With benches and public displays
A good place to relax
A good place to do everything.
